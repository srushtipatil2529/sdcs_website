About Our Equipment Services

Crawler Crane

Designed for exceptional stability, our crawler cranes excel on challenging terrains, making them perfect for infrastructure, power plants, and heavy industrial projects. each crane is rigorously maintained to ensure peak efficiency and safety. Shri Dinesh Crane Services combines cutting-edge technology with experienced operators to provide customized, dependable solutions for every project.

Equipment Maintenance

we regularly perform oil changes, replace oil and air filters, and grease components every 700 hours to ensure lubrication and reduce wear, while also inspecting tyres and crawler tracks for pressure, tread, tension, and damage. Service engines every 500 km (700 hours), checking it's hoses, fluids, and the camera diesel sensor for optimal performance. At our organization we keep accounting of maintenance activities meticulously to track performance and comply with manufacturer guidelines. Synchronize 700-hours tasks to minimize downtime. We regularly review logs to anticipate future needs and prevent failures.

Operator Safety Management

Shri Dinesh Crane Services prioritizes operator safety and management by providing comprehensive training and essential safety gear, including helmets, gloves, and jackets, for crane operators. The company ensures the medical well-being of its operators through mandatory eye tests, blood tests, and full body check-ups. To maintain operational efficiency, a single shift includes one operator, while a double shift includes two operators and more than two helpers. Additionally, the company offers competitive salaries, a provident fund of ₹20,72,570, and Employee State Insurance Corporation benefits amounting to ₹57,947, ensuring financial and health security for its workforce.
